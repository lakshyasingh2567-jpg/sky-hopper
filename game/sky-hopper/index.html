About Sky Hopper

Sky Hopper is a free online arcade jumping game where your goal is to stay alive by landing on platforms, collecting coins, and avoiding falls. The game is easy to start but gets harder as your score increases.

How to Play

The character jumps automatically. Your job is to move left and right so the character lands safely on the next platform.

Controls

  • Desktop: Use A/D or Arrow Keys.
  • Mobile: Use the left and right buttons on the screen.

Tips & Tricks

  • Stay near the center of the screen so you have more time to react.
  • Collect coins when they are safe, but do not risk falling for every coin.
  • Watch the platform gaps carefully as the game becomes faster.

Game Features

  • Endless arcade gameplay
  • Coin collection
  • High score tracking
  • Mobile-friendly controls
  • Sound effects and background music
