About Sky Hopper

Sky Hopper is a free online arcade jumping game where your goal is to stay alive by landing on platforms, collecting coins, and avoiding falls.

How to Play

The character jumps automatically. You only need to control the movement left and right.

Controls

  • Desktop: Use A/D or Arrow Keys.
  • Mobile: Use the left and right buttons on the screen.

Game Features

  • Endless arcade gameplay
  • Coin collection
  • High score tracking
