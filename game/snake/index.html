About Snake

Snake is a classic arcade game where you control a growing snake. Eat apples, increase your score, and avoid crashing into walls or your own body.

How to Play

Use the arrow keys on desktop or the on-screen buttons on mobile. The longer your snake gets, the harder the game becomes.
