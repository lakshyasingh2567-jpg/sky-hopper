About Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a classic puzzle game where two players take turns placing X and O on a 3×3 board. The first player to get three marks in a row wins.

How to Play

Player X starts first. Click any empty square to place your mark. Try to complete a row, column, or diagonal before your opponent.
