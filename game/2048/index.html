About 2048

2048 is a classic number puzzle game where you slide tiles around the board and combine matching numbers. The goal is to create the 2048 tile while keeping space open for future moves.

Tips to Win

  • Keep your largest tile in one corner.
  • Avoid random moves.
  • Try to keep similar numbers close together.
  • Plan your next move before swiping.
